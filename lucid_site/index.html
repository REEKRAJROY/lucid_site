Who are we?


At Lucid Education Group we focus on bridging the gap between Industry and the Academics, using our informative modules and classes for students to learn and grow the skills which the industry require them to have, And Exploiting our Industrial links for students to refine and hone their skills while having a hands- on approach to the challenges and tasks offered by the Industry. We are building our Market Training Program that we plan to launch for the Tier 2, Tier 3 College Students so as to provide them a real-time industry exposure.

LEMT PROGRAM

LEMT is a 55-60 hour Internship Program spread over 4-6 weeks for the college students which are delivered by Lucid Edu Group and their Partner Companies through Pre Recorded Videos, Live Sessions, Quizzes, Pre Reads, Podcasts, Desired Mobile Applications, Articles, and Open-Source Material awarding an Internship letter and Market Training certificates to the students post successful completion.
